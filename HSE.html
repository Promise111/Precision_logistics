We Are Precision!!

Precision Logistics Limited(PLL), a subsidiary of Fluid Management Services(FMS) Limited was incorporated as a Limited Liability
Company under the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria in May 2005 and commenced business immediately.
We are an Engineering Support and Procurement service company. We provide Procurement, Maintenance,
Calibration, Manpower, Inspection of Lifting Equipment and other Engineering Support Services to
major Oil & Gas Industry in Nigeria and West Africa.

HSE Policy

Precision Logistics Limited (PLL) is committed to protecting the health and safety of our employees, our customers, the public and the environment in which we operate. In PLL it is our policy to conduct our business in a manner consistent with all applicable health, safety and environmental laws and regulations. In furtherance of this policy PLL will endeavour to: Recognize that HSE is everybody’s direct responsibility. Set and maintain high standards of health and safety at all locations. Comply with applicable health, safety and environmental laws and regulations at work place or client’s site. Create HSE awareness amongst all employees through program of training & education. Monitor and continually improve HSE performance
